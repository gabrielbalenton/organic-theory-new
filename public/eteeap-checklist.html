Heads-up · Las Piñas IDs vs. Nuvali residence

Get your location-based documents where you live

Your IDs show Las Piñas but you live in Nuvali, Laguna. This matters for the Cedula (#4), a Barangay Clearance (#5), and the Notarized Service Record (#6). Secure these from your Nuvali / Sta. Rosa / Calamba barangay or city hall using proof of residence — no need to match the Las Piñas ID address. If any office questions it, an optional notarized Affidavit of "One and the Same Person" resolves it.
