The Single Biggest Opportunity

Rename the site from "mysite" and retitle all three indexed pages.

This isn't a rewrite — it's a settings fix. Renaming the Wix site once and adding Longs, SC plus your core services to each page title fixes the Home, About, and Contact pages in one pass, putting your own name and location into every search result Google shows for you.
