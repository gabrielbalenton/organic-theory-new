Growth Audit — Brittle Family Law Group

Your consultation button does nothing when clicked.

Brittle Family Law Group has strong reviews, real credentials, and meaningful content — but a handful of technical issues are silently sending potential clients to competitors before they ever reach your phone.

What I found on brittle-law.com

✗

Your primary CTA button is broken

The main "Request a Consultation" button in your hero section links to # — it goes nowhere. Any potential client who clicks it lands right back at the top of the page, confused. This is your single highest-priority fix.

✗

Images are broken throughout the site

Multiple image assets are failing to load and displaying as SVG placeholders — including attorney photos. For a firm where trust is the primary conversion driver, missing headshots and visuals undermine credibility immediately.

✗

Contact form is buried at the very bottom

A client in crisis — dealing with a divorce or custody situation — needs to reach you within seconds of landing. Your form requires scrolling past the entire page. Most of them won't make it.

⚠

No local schema markup

There's no LocalBusiness or LegalService schema on your site. This means Google can't surface your firm in rich snippets for "family law attorney Portland" — a search your ideal clients make every day.

⚠

Video library referenced but appears empty

Your navigation references a Video Library, but there's no content behind it. A video library that actually delivers builds enormous trust — leaving it empty signals to Google (and clients) that the site is neglected.

The single biggest opportunity

Fix the broken CTA and move a contact form above the fold. You already have the trust signals — Super Lawyers badge, AVVO 10.0, real 5-star reviews. The only thing standing between your credentials and a new client is a working button and a form they can actually find. That's a days-long fix, not a redesign.

What I'd fix in the first 2 weeks

  1. 1. Fix the broken CTA — connect it to an inline consultation form or Calendly embed.
  2. 2. Add a contact form or callback widget above the fold, visible within 3 seconds of landing.
  3. 3. Audit and fix all broken image assets — attorney headshots in particular are critical for trust.
  4. 4. Add LegalService + LocalBusiness schema markup to capture Portland "near me" searches.
  5. 5. Populate or remove the Video Library — either commit to content or clean up the navigation.
