The Single Biggest Opportunity

Retire the old S3 bucket, consolidate the rest.

Being one of only a handful of co-op climbing gyms in Canada, run by members since 2010, is a genuinely rare story — right now Google is splitting that story between your real domain and a dead, unbranded Amazon S3 address. Redirecting or de-indexing the old bucket is a same-week fix.

What I'd Fix In The First Week

  1. 01Take the old S3-hosted site offline, or at minimum block it from indexing and 301-redirect it to kingstonboulderingcoop.com.
  2. 02Request removal of the old AWS URL from Google's index via Search Console so it stops surfacing at all.
  3. 03Build a dedicated "Own A Share" page around the $10 co-op story — it's a genuine differentiator with no page of its own.
  4. 04Add LocalBusiness schema so Kingston-area searchers see hours and drop-in pricing as rich results.
  5. 05Resubmit a clean sitemap so Google re-crawls the co-op as one consolidated site.
