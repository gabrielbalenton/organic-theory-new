The Single Biggest Opportunity

Kill the duplicate Services page, clean up the ugly URLs.

Close to 100 years of combined craft is real proof — right now it's split between a real Services page and an unbranded duplicate, with four more pages sitting on nonsense URLs. A handful of redirects and renamed slugs consolidates that signal onto pages that actually represent the workshop.

What I'd Fix In The First Week

  1. 01301-redirect /home-2-2-2-2-2-2/ into the real /our-services/ page.
  2. 02Rename Gallery, Print & Design, Sustainability, and Courses to clean, descriptive URLs.
  3. 03Add self-referencing canonical tags across the site so this can't recur.
  4. 04Submit the corrected URL structure to Google Search Console for re-crawl.
  5. 05Add LocalBusiness schema so Penge and South London searches show hours and address as rich results.
