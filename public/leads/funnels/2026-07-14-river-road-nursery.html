What I Found On Your Site

01
✗

The account login popup says "Rubnio," not River Road

Click Login/Register and the popup greets returning shoppers with "Already a Rubnio Customer?" — Rubnio is the name of the WordPress theme the site was built on, never swapped out for your actual name. It's a small thing, but it's the first thing a returning customer sees.

02
⚠

The homepage is a product grid with no written story

The banner just repeats "THE NURSERY" across two slides, then drops straight into "Indoor Plants (102)" and "New Zealand Native (122)" category tiles. There's no line anywhere on the homepage saying you're family-owned, based in Tuakau, or a native plant specialist — a first-time visitor has to dig for it.

03
⚠

No meta description on the homepage title tag

When someone searches "plant nursery Tuakau" or "buy native plants NZ," Google has no hand-written snippet to show — it falls back to auto-generating one from whatever text it finds first.

The Single Biggest Opportunity

Rebrand the login popup, add a real homepage story, and write a proper meta description.

Being family-owned and NZ-native-specialist is a real edge over the big box garden centres — right now the site doesn't say either. Fixing the leftover theme text and adding a short "why us" section is a same-week job with a real trust payoff.

What I'd Fix In The First Week
