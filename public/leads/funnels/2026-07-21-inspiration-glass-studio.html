One current address, everywhere.

That's not a content problem, it's a directory cleanup, and it's exactly the kind of thing that costs you a walk-in student or custom-order client before they ever see the studio.

What I'd Fix In The First 2-3 Weeks

  1. 01Claim and correct all three CMac.ws listings to show 40212 Industrial Park Cir STE 101, matching your site and Yelp.
