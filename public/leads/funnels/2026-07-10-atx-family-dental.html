Growth Audit — ATX Family Dental

Your site reads like every other dentist in Austin — and Google can't tell you apart.

ATX Family Dental has real differentiators as an independent practice on S. 1st Street — but your homepage doesn't say any of them, and Google has nothing to index that would put you ahead of the corporate chains.

What I found on your site

✗

No meta description on your homepage

When someone searches "dentist Austin 78704," Google has nothing specific to show in the results snippet — it'll auto-generate one, usually poorly. This directly hurts your click-through rate before a visitor ever reaches your site.

✗

No schema markup for your practice

There's no structured data telling Google you're a dental practice with a specific address, hours, and services. That means you're invisible to rich results (star ratings, hours, "open now" badges) that corporate chains like Aspen Dental already show.

⚠

Homepage copy doesn't say what makes you different

The main line is "A healthy mouth is the cornerstone of overall physical wellness" — true, but generic. It could be any dental site in the country. Nothing here tells a South Austin parent why to pick you over the practice two blocks away.

⚠

No reviews or testimonials on the homepage

As an independent practice, patient trust is your biggest asset against the corporate chains — but there's no social proof shown where new visitors actually land.

The single biggest opportunity

Add schema markup and real reviews, and rewrite the homepage around what's actually true about ATX Family Dental. Independent practices win on trust and personal care — right now your site doesn't make that case anywhere. Fixing the technical SEO gap plus the messaging gap together is what moves you up past the corporate chains in local search.

What I'd fix in the first 2 weeks

  1. 1. Write a real meta description targeting "dentist South Austin 78704."
  2. 2. Add LocalBusiness + Dentist schema markup so Google can show hours, ratings, and rich results.
  3. 3. Rewrite the homepage hero around your actual differentiators — independent, personalized, S. 1st Street location.
  4. 4. Pull in and display real Google reviews on the homepage.
  5. 5. Add descriptive alt text to every banner and team image.
