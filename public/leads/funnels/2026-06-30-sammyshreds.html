What I found on your site

No meta description

When someone searches "personal trainer Adelaide" and your site appears, Google shows a random snippet instead of a compelling description. Fewer people click. You lose the lead before they even visit.

⚙️

Three steps to enquire

Questionnaire → phone call → consultation. Each extra step loses 30–40% of potential clients. Most people visiting your site are ready to commit — they just need an easy path.

💬

Vague service descriptions

"Plans" with no pricing, no package breakdown, and no comparison table. Visitors can't evaluate whether you're right for them — so they leave to find someone who makes it clearer.

🏆

Trust signals buried below fold

Your credentials and client results don't appear until well down the page. First impressions decide in 3 seconds — your qualifications need to be visible immediately.

Within 60 days: more organic clicks from Google, a measurable increase in direct consultation bookings, and a homepage that converts visitors into paying clients — not just visitors.
