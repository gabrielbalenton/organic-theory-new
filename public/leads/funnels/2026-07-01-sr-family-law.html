Growth Opportunity Report

SR Family Law has strong foundations — but Google isn't showing potential clients why they should choose you.

Your firm is competing in one of Queensland's most active legal markets. The traffic is there. The gaps below are preventing it from converting into enquiries.

What we found on your site

🔍

No meta description in Google search results

When someone searches "family lawyer Gold Coast" and sees your result, there is no description underneath your link. Google pulls random text from the page instead. This means you're competing on title alone, while firms with a well-written description give searchers a clear reason to click. Writing one compelling sentence here is the highest-ROI fix available right now.

📄

Homepage content is thin — service pages are doing the heavy lifting

Your homepage primarily navigates visitors to other pages rather than building the case for why SR Family Law is the right choice. Google rewards pages with substantive, unique content. A homepage that explains your specific approach, outcomes you've achieved for clients, and what separates you from other Gold Coast family lawyers would rank better and convert more visitors.

💬

Testimonials use initials only, reducing trust

Your existing reviews are positive, but displaying them as initials ("S.R.", "M.L.") makes them feel anonymous and easy to fabricate. Prospects under stress from a separation or custody dispute need to feel confident they're in safe hands. Full first names, photos where possible, or a direct link to verified Google reviews would significantly increase the trust value of your testimonials.

📝

Blog exists but isn't driving traffic

Family law is a high-anxiety, question-heavy area. People searching "how does property settlement work in Queensland" or "what are my rights as a parent after separation" are exactly your future clients. A content strategy targeting these questions with clear, helpful answers could bring hundreds of qualified visitors to your site each month at zero ongoing cost.

The single most impactful improvement

Writing optimized meta descriptions for your key service pages and homepage. It's a one-hour fix that immediately changes how your firm looks in every Google search result, and it compounds as Google's data confirms higher click-through rates.

Realistic outcome within 30 days: measurably higher click-through rate from search. Within 60 days: more initial enquiry calls from people who found you organically.
