Growth Audit — Sentinel Pest Control

Your homepage is emailing leads to the wrong company.

Sentinel Pest Control has real credentials in Kitchener-Waterloo — a Gold Award and a top-3 local ranking — but one line of homepage copy is quietly sending real inquiries somewhere else entirely.

What I Found On Your Site

01
✗

Your homepage sends emails to a different company

In the "Leaders in the Pest Control Industry" section, the site says "email us at info@sentinelpestcontrol.com" — but that domain belongs to a completely unrelated pest control company in Springfield, Illinois, founded in 1932. Anyone who clicks that link sends their inquiry there instead of to your real inbox at info@sentinelpestcontrol.ca.

02
✗

Alt text left in Spanish, from a stock photo library

Your cockroach service photo's alt text still reads "Una cucaracha en fondo blanco" ("a cockroach on white background") — the raw caption from the stock photo site, never replaced with real English copy about your service.

03
⚠

Another photo caption is cut off mid-word

Your bed bug service photo's alt text reads "Bed bug Cimex lectularius parasitic insects of the cimicid famil" — "family" gets cut off. None of your service photos are eligible to show up in Google Image Search the way they're captioned now.

04
⚠

Your Gold Award and 2024 ranking aren't on the homepage

The site mentions a Gold Award and a "1 of 3 Best Pest Control in Kitchener 2024" ranking, but there's no schema or review markup connecting that credibility to your actual Google listing.
