Growth Audit — Fluff Haven

Google calls both your pages "Mysite".

You've built a groomer clients keep coming back to, year after year, right here in Georgetown. But your own name doesn't appear once in the two pages Google actually shows for your site.

What I Found On Your Site

01
✗

Homepage is indexed as "Home | Mysite"

Google shows your homepage — the very first thing anyone sees when they search for you — as "Home | Mysite." "Mysite" is the placeholder name every new Wix site ships with, and it was never renamed. Someone searching "Fluff Haven Georgetown" sees no confirmation they've found the right business.

02
✗

Services page has the same problem

Your "My Services" page — the page someone checks to see what a groom actually includes before booking — is indexed as "My Services | Mysite." Same issue, second page. Your name isn't attached to either listing Google has for you.

03
⚠

Nothing distinguishes you on the wixsite.com address

The site still lives on a shared fluffhaven.wixsite.com/mysite address instead of a domain of your own, and no title anywhere mentions Georgetown, TX — so there's no local signal for someone searching "dog groomer Georgetown."

The Single Biggest Opportunity

Put your own name back on your own pages.

Right now, both pages Google has indexed for your site — Home and My Services — show "Mysite" instead of Fluff Haven in the exact line Google displays. That's an easy, same-day fix in the Wix site settings, and it's the highest-leverage change available: it costs nothing to implement and changes what every searcher sees before they click.

What I'd Fix In The First 2 Weeks

  1. 01Rename the Wix site title from "Mysite" to "Fluff Haven" so it stops overriding your brand on both indexed pages.
  2. 02Rewrite the homepage and Services page titles to lead with "Fluff Haven" plus Georgetown, TX.
  3. 03Add LocalBusiness + PetGroomer schema markup so your rating and hours can show directly in search results.
  4. 04Write a real meta description for both pages instead of leaving them to Wix's default.
  5. 05Move off the shared wixsite.com address onto a domain of your own to match the trust you've already earned with regulars.
