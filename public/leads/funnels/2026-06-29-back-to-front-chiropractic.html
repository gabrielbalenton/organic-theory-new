What I found on your site

  • ✗ Homepage title tag: Shows "Contact phone Number 3393 2201" in Google results — zero keyword value, very low click-through rate.
  • ✗ No meta description: Google auto-generates a random snippet — often a garbled sentence, not a compelling reason to click.
  • ✗ Broken currency display: Your site shows GBP (£) pricing for an Australian clinic — looks like a plugin error, damages trust immediately.
  • ✗ No local schema markup: No structured data for your opening hours, location, or healthcare service — invisible to Google's rich result features.
  • ✗ Missing image alt text: Logo and key images have no alt attributes — accessibility issue and missed keyword signals.

The single biggest win

Fixing your title tag and meta description alone could increase organic click-through by 20–35% without changing a single other thing on the site. Pair that with local healthcare schema markup and a cleaned-up booking flow, and your clinic starts showing up in Google's Local Pack for "chiropractor near me" searches — the highest-intent traffic available.

With 5 stars across 62 genuine reviews, you already have the trust. The problem is Google can not properly read your site to show it to the right people.

What I'd fix, in order

  1. Rewrite title tag and meta description with local keywords and a clear value prop
  2. Remove or disable the broken WooCommerce/cart plugin showing GBP pricing
  3. Add LocalBusiness + MedicalBusiness schema for Google rich results
  4. Add alt text to all images and fix duplicate nav markup
  5. Create a mobile-sticky booking CTA (reduces friction on the #1 search device)

Realistic outcome

Within 60 days: higher search ranking for "chiropractor East Brisbane," more qualified clicks from Google, and a cleaner booking experience that converts first-time visitors into appointments. No ads spend required.
