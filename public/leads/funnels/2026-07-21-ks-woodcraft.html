The Single Biggest Opportunity

One address, one set of hours, everywhere.

A family-run McKinney furniture shop is currently sending mixed signals about where it even is - Foursquare and the Chamber of Commerce list an address on North Tennessee St, while your most-reviewed listing on Yelp places it on South Tennessee St. Hours are inconsistent too. None of this needs new content - it's a directory cleanup, and it's exactly the kind of thing that costs you a walk-in customer before they ever see your work.
