What I Found On Your Site

01
✗

The email link at the top of every page displays "info@vacsew.com.com"

The mailto address itself works fine, but the visible link text in your header — the very first thing anyone reads before the logo even loads — has a doubled ".com.com" typo. It's a small thing that undercuts the "60 years, family-run" trust you've actually earned.

02
✗

"Independant" is misspelled in your own pitch against big-box stores

The homepage teaser reads "Why purchase your next vacuum cleaner from an Independant Vacuum Cleaner Store?" — the exact word making your case against Best Buy and Amazon is spelled wrong, right on your own homepage.

03
⚠

No structured data despite 60 years in one location

"Same location since 1966" is exactly the kind of longevity Google rewards with rich local results — but without LocalBusiness schema tying it to your Hawthorne Blvd address and hours, none of that shows up in search.

04
⚠

Real 5-star testimonials aren't marked up for rich results

You already have genuine, specific customer quotes from Torrance, Palos Verdes, and Hermosa Beach on the homepage — real social proof most competitors don't have — but there's no review schema, so none of it can surface as star ratings in Google.
