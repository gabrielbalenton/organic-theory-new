The Single Biggest Opportunity

Give your homepage the same care as the rest of your site.

Every other indexed page follows a clean, consistent "[Page] — Luthier On Call" title. The homepage — the page that gets the most search traffic by far — is the one page that breaks the pattern, with a run-on title and no location. That's a same-day fix with real upside.

What I'd Fix In The First Week

  1. 01Rewrite the homepage title to match the site's own established pattern, with Pompano Beach, FL added explicitly.
  2. 02Write a real meta description for the homepage — right now Google is likely pulling a snippet from body text at random.
  3. 03Add LocalBusiness schema so Pompano Beach-area searchers see hours and location as rich results.
  4. 04Resubmit the homepage in Search Console and monitor impressions for "violin repair Pompano Beach."
