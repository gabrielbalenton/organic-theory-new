Growth Audit — Born & Bred Studio

Some of your portfolio links lead nowhere.

Born & Bred Studio has genuinely strong testimonials — but several of the portfolio project links a prospective client would click to see your actual work are broken, and there's no full address for Google to associate you with London searches.

What I found on your site

✗

Several portfolio project links are broken

Links like "/crouchendvictorianterrace" are missing their full domain path, and the slider navigation contains empty link references. A prospective client trying to browse your past work can hit a dead end mid-click.

⚠

No meta description

Nothing tells Google what to show for "interior designer London" — you're leaving your search snippet to chance.

⚠

Incomplete address in footer

Your footer only lists "London, UK" — no street address or postcode. That makes it harder for Google to confidently associate your business with local London searches.

⚠

No schema markup

No structured data for your business or services, missing an opportunity for richer search results.

⚠

Portfolio and testimonial images have no alt text

Your strongest asset — the work itself — isn't contributing to image search visibility because none of the photos have descriptive alt text.

The single biggest opportunity

Fix the broken portfolio links first. Your testimonials are genuinely strong ("Lisa was an absolute dream to work with") — but if a prospective client can't actually click through and see the finished projects behind those quotes, you're losing the exact moment that turns interest into a consultation booking.
