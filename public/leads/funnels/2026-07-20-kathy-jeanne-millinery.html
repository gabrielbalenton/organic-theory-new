The Single Biggest Opportunity

Consolidate two homepages and a duplicate contact page into one clean structure.

A 40-year heritage millinery house is currently splitting its own search presence between a duplicate homepage, a "-2" contact page, and a mix of http and https product pages. Consolidating each duplicate into a single canonical page, and forcing https sitewide, is a fast fix with a real payoff for a shop working with century-old tooling.
