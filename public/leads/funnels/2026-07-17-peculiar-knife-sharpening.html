The Single Biggest Opportunity

Build dedicated Pricing and Service Area pages from what you already do.

Nothing here requires reinventing your service — it requires giving Google two more pages to actually index: one answering "how much does this cost," and one built around "Kansas City" search terms. Right now both questions have no page to land on, so they go to whoever does have one.
