The Single Biggest Opportunity

Consolidate onto one clean site, and let it rank on its own.

Right now you're maintaining, and search engines are indexing, three separate web presences for one studio. Fixing the URL structure and retiring the old Wix site puts all your search authority behind the one site that actually represents you today.

What I'd Fix First

  1. 01301-redirect the old Wix site and the subdomain into the one real site so search engines stop splitting authority.
  2. 02Rebuild the page structure with clean URLs — /tattoos, /piercing, /vouchers instead of index.php query strings.
  3. 03Write titles and descriptions that lead with the vegan-ink specialty and Blackrock location.
  4. 04Add LocalBusiness schema so hours, address, and reviews can show directly in search.
  5. 05Add descriptive alt text to portfolio and studio photos across the site.
