Growth Audit — Prime Piano Services

Google has your own homepage indexed twice.

As a Certified and Registered Piano Technician covering eight North Metro Atlanta counties, you've built real credentials — but a leftover duplicate page is quietly splitting the ranking signal your homepage should be building on its own.

What I Found On Your Site

01
✗

Your homepage is indexed at two separate addresses

Google has both www.primepianoservices.com and primepianoservices.com/index.html indexed as live, separate pages — the same homepage content sitting at two different URLs. That splits whatever ranking signal your homepage builds instead of consolidating it on one address.

02
⚠

No canonical tag pointing search engines to one version

Without a canonical URL declared, Google has to guess which of the two indexed homepage addresses is the "real" one — and in the meantime, both versions compete against each other instead of one clear page building authority.

03
⚠

Your CPT/RPT credential isn't in the homepage title

Being a Certified and Registered Piano Technician is a real, checkable credential most piano tuners in the Atlanta area can't claim — it's mentioned on your About page, but it doesn't appear in the title Google actually shows for your homepage.

The Single Biggest Opportunity

Consolidate onto one canonical homepage.

Right now Google splits your homepage's ranking power between two indexed addresses. A single redirect and canonical tag puts all of that behind one page — and gives you a natural moment to lead with the CPT/RPT credential that sets you apart.

What I'd Fix In The First 2 Weeks

  1. 01301 redirect /index.html to the canonical www homepage.
  2. 02Add a canonical tag to every page pointing to its single correct URL.
  3. 03Rewrite the homepage title to lead with "Certified Piano Technician."
  4. 04Add LocalBusiness + Service schema covering all eight counties served.
  5. 05Resubmit the corrected sitemap to Search Console so it recrawls cleanly.
