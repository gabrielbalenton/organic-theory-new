Growth Audit — Baker & Spice

Your homepage says "welcome" — and nothing else.

Baker & Spice has a loyal SW Portland following, real weekly ordering, and seasonal specials like Father's Day ordering — but your homepage copy is a single generic welcome line with no mention of what you actually bake.

What I found on your site

✗

Homepage copy is one generic line

The entire main message is "Welcome to Baker & Spice! We're glad you're here." There's no mention of your pastry specialties, your neighborhood story, or why someone in SW Portland should choose you over another bakery.

⚠

No meta description

Nothing tells Google what to show for "bakery SW Portland" — you're leaving your search snippet to chance.

⚠

No schema markup

No structured data for your hours, address, or business type — a missed opportunity given you already have clear, consistent NAP info that's easy to mark up.

⚠

Logo and footer image have no alt text

A quick accessibility and SEO fix that's currently missing.

The single biggest opportunity

Rewrite the homepage copy around what you actually make. You clearly have real seasonal offerings (Father's Day ordering, daily ordering) and a real neighborhood presence — none of that shows up on the homepage right now. A specific, appetite-driving description plus basic schema markup is a fast, low-cost fix with real upside for foot traffic from search.

What I'd fix in the first 2 weeks

  1. 1. Rewrite the homepage hero copy around your actual pastry specialties and neighborhood story.
  2. 2. Write a meta description targeting "bakery SW Portland."
  3. 3. Add LocalBusiness + Bakery schema markup with your hours and address.
  4. 4. Add descriptive alt text to the logo and footer images.
