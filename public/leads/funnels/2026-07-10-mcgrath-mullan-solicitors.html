Growth Audit — McGrath Mullan Solicitors

Your navigation menu repeats 5 times in the page code.

McGrath Mullan positions itself as a small-business-friendly firm — but the site's homepage leans on abstract taglines instead of practice areas, and the page itself is bloated with a navigation menu duplicated five separate times.

What I found on your site

✗

Navigation menu duplicated 5+ times

The site's navigation appears repeatedly throughout the page source — this bloats load time and can confuse screen readers and search engine crawlers alike.

⚠

Homepage copy is abstract, not specific

The opening section leans on taglines like "Justice," "Equality," "Dignity" — inspiring, but it doesn't tell a small business owner which of your services actually applies to their problem.

⚠

No meta description or schema markup

Nothing tells Google what to show for solicitor searches in Dublin or Oldcastle, and no structured data means you're not eligible for richer local search results.

⚠

Inconsistent phone number formatting

Phone numbers appear in both +353 and 049 prefix formats across the site — a small but real consistency issue for local search verification.

⚠

Lawyer and team photos have no alt text

A quick accessibility and SEO fix currently missing across your team page.

The single biggest opportunity

Clean up the technical bloat and rewrite the homepage around your actual practice areas. You already position yourselves as understanding "the unique needs and challenges" of small businesses — the homepage just needs to say that in plain terms instead of abstract taglines, backed by a faster, cleaner page.
