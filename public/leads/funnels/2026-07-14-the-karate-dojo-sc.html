Growth Audit — The Karate Dojo

Google has nothing to show for "West Columbia karate".

Your homepage doesn't have a meta description at all, and nowhere on the page does it say "West Columbia" — even though your own Facebook page is literally named WestColumbiaDojo.

What I Found On Your Site

01
✗

No meta description on your homepage

There's no description tag at all — when a parent searches "karate classes West Columbia," Google has nothing written for your listing and auto-generates one instead, usually pulling in whatever text happens to be nearby.

02
✗

Your page title never mentions West Columbia

The homepage title is just "The Karate Dojo." Your Facebook page is literally named "WestColumbiaDojo" — that local signal exists everywhere except the one place Google weighs most.

03
⚠

Your Twitter link is broken

The Twitter icon in your footer links to "www.twitter.com/https://twitter.com/TheKarateDojoSC" — a malformed double URL that 404s instead of reaching your account.

04
⚠

Several PDF links still use unsecured http://

Your class schedule, newsletter, and rank test PDFs all link out over plain http:// while the rest of the site runs on https:// — mixed content that some browsers flag or quietly block.
