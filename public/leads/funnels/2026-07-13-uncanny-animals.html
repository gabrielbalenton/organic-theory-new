The Single Biggest Opportunity

Put "Yarraville" and "Melbourne" back into your own title tags.

You've already done the hard part — the reviews, the certifications, the reputation. This is a small, mechanical fix: every page title just needs a location. It's the fastest-ROI change on this whole list.

What I'd Fix In The First Week

  1. 01Rewrite the homepage title to lead with "Dog & Cat Grooming in Yarraville."
  2. 02Add "Yarraville" or "Melbourne's Inner West" to every inner page title — About, Price Guide, Grooming, Bookings, FAQ.
  3. 03Add LocalBusiness + PetGroomer schema so your 4.9-star rating can show directly in search results.
  4. 04Write meta descriptions that lead with fear-free certification and vegan products — your real differentiators.
  5. 05Add descriptive alt text to grooming and boutique photos across the site.
