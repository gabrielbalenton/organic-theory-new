Growth Audit — The Dental Practice

Your site still thinks it's 2022.

The Dental Practice has served Botany and Dannemora families for over 20 years — but a stale pandemic-era banner and an orphaned duplicate homepage are the first things a new patient and Google both see.

What I Found On Your Site

01
✗

A COVID-19 traffic-light banner is still live on your homepage

Your homepage currently reads: "We are OPEN normally at the Orange Traffic Light setting of the COVID-19 Protection Framework..." with a link to a "COVID-19 Protocols" page. New Zealand retired the traffic light framework back in 2022. To anyone researching a dentist in 2026, this is the first thing they see — and it reads as a site nobody has touched in years, before they've even booked.

02
✗

A leftover pandemic-era duplicate homepage is still live and indexable

There's a separate page still sitting on your own domain at /home-2-1, titled "Home (Copy Alert Level 4)" — an old duplicate homepage from the COVID alert-level era that was never cleaned up or removed. Beyond looking abandoned, a leftover duplicate like this on a live domain is a real duplicate-content flag search engines can penalize.

03
⚠

No schema markup for your practice

There's no structured data telling Google you're a dental clinic with a specific address, hours, and services. You're invisible to rich results (star ratings, hours, "open now" badges) that patients use to decide who to call first.

04
⚠

No reviews or testimonials on the homepage

After 20+ years serving Botany and Dannemora families, that trust is your biggest asset — but nothing on your homepage shows it to a first-time visitor comparing you against every other Auckland practice.
