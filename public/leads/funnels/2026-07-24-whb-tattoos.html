The Single Biggest Opportunity

Put your studio's name on its own search listing.

Right now, the only page Google has indexed for your studio shows "mysite" instead of WHB Tattoos in the exact line a searcher sees. That's an easy, same-day fix in the Wix site settings, and it's the highest-leverage change available — it costs nothing to implement and changes what every searcher sees before they click.

What I'd Fix In The First 2 Weeks

  1. 01Rename the Wix site title from "mysite" to "WHB Tattoos" so it stops overriding your brand in search.
  2. 02Rewrite the homepage title to lead with "WHB Tattoos" plus your service area.
  3. 03Add a proper meta description so your listing tells searchers what you actually offer, not a generic guess.
  4. 04Build out separate Portfolio and About pages so more than one page of the site can be found in search.
  5. 05Move off the shared wixsite.com address onto a domain of your own.
