The Single Biggest Opportunity

A title that actually says what you are.

A shop with 30+ years of embroidery experience is currently telling Google — and everyone who searches — nothing more than "website." That's the single line most people see before ever clicking through, and right now it doesn't even say what business this is. Fixing it is a rewrite, not a rebuild.

What I'd Fix In The First 2-3 Weeks

  1. 01Rewrite the homepage title tag to lead with "Palm Beach Embroidery" plus "Palm Beach Gardens, FL," dropping the generic "website" title entirely.
  2. 02Noindex the leftover default "Sample Page" so it stops competing with your real pages in search.
  3. 03Write a real meta description mentioning embroidery, apparel decoration, and Palm Beach County so the search snippet actually invites a click.
  4. 04Add LocalBusiness schema with the Military Trail address so Google has one clean, structured source of truth.
