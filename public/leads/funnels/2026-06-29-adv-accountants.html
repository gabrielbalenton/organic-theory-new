What I found on your site

  • ✗ Only 2 pages indexed: Google has discovered almost nothing of your site — services, about, and location pages are not being crawled or ranked.
  • ✗ No meta description on any page: Google writes its own snippets for your listings, which are often irrelevant and reduce click-through rates.
  • ✗ Stale copyright (2017): Visible in the footer — sends an immediate signal that the site is not actively maintained, which affects trust and rankings.
  • ✗ Weak H1 headline: "Welcome to ADV Partners Chartered Accountants" contains no searchable keywords — "tax accountant Sunshine VIC" or similar would pull in local search traffic.
  • ✗ No local SEO signals: Two offices, zero Google Business Profile optimization visible — local map pack visibility is essentially zero.

The single biggest win

Getting Google to properly index your site and optimising for local search terms like "tax accountant Sunshine VIC" and "accountant Lalor" would put you in front of people actively searching for your services in your exact service areas — at zero ongoing ad spend.

A sitemap submission, crawl fix, and two location-specific pages could realistically move you from invisible to page one within 90 days in these low-competition local markets.

What I'd fix, in order

  1. Audit and fix indexation — remove the duplicate /contact-2/ and submit a proper XML sitemap
  2. Rewrite all title tags and meta descriptions with local keyword targeting
  3. Build dedicated location pages for Sunshine and Lalor (each targeting suburb-specific terms)
  4. Optimise and verify Google Business Profiles for both locations
  5. Add a clear lead capture form above the fold — not just a phone number

Realistic outcome
