Growth Audit — Walton's Family Auto Service

Google sees two different addresses for your one shop.

Walton's has 50 years of real trust built in Garner — but your site shows your address in two different formats between the header and footer, which quietly works against you every time Google tries to verify your business location.

What I found on your site

✗

Inconsistent business address (NAP)

Your header shows "101 St. Mary's Street, Garner, NC" (no zip code) while your footer shows "101 St. Mary's Street Garner, NC 27529." Search engines cross-check this kind of detail — inconsistency like this quietly undermines your local SEO trust signal.

⚠

No meta description

Nothing tells Google what to show for "auto repair Garner NC" — you're leaving your search snippet to chance.

⚠

No schema markup

No structured data for your business, hours, or services — a missed opportunity for rich results given your real, verifiable 50-year track record.

⚠

Homepage slideshow images have no alt text

All 8 images in your homepage slideshow are missing alt text — a quick accessibility and SEO fix.

The single biggest opportunity

Fix the address inconsistency and add schema markup. You've got 50 years of real, earned trust in Garner — the technical side of your site just isn't backing that up yet. This is a genuinely quick fix with a real payoff for local search visibility.
