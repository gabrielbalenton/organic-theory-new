The Single Biggest Opportunity

Clean up the "/us/" URLs, and name your bikes in their titles.

A local shop with a real repair reputation is currently splitting its indexed pages across two URL patterns, with product pages that don't name the actual bike. Fixing the structure and titles is fast, with a real payoff for anyone searching by model.

What I'd Fix In The First 2-3 Weeks

  1. 01301 redirect the "/us/service" and "/us/bikes-*" pages onto clean, prefix-free URLs.
  2. 02Give each bike model page its own descriptive title, naming the actual model.
  3. 03Rewrite the Repair Services page title to lead with "bike repair" and Livermore/East Bay.
  4. 04Add LocalBusiness schema so hours, address, and rating can show as rich results.
  5. 05Give tune-ups and tire/tube service each a short, indexable description block.
