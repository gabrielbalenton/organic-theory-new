What I Found On Your Site

✗

A 2019 birth announcement is still one of your top indexed pages

The page titled "Baby Alpaca EBONY - born June 16, 2019" is still one of Google's top picks for frostfarm.net — ahead of anything about your current herd, this year's crias, or your shop. To a searcher, it reads like the farm hasn't had news since 2019.

02
⚠

Two separate pages cover the same farm history

"Farm History" and "History" (Through the Years) both cover the same background material as separate, indexed pages — splitting whatever relevance either one could build on its own instead of consolidating into one authoritative page.

03
⚠

The farm store and shop aren't clearly separated for search

"Farm Store" and "Products" (Shop) both exist as separate sections without a clear distinction Google — or a first-time visitor — can use to tell what's sold where.
