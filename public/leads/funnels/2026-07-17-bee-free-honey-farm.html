What I Found On Your Site

✗

Your homepage title has no location in it

The title Google indexes right now is "Home | Nucleus Colonies by Bee Free Honey Farm" — it leads with the generic word "Home" and never mentions Rio Rancho or New Mexico, even though you're a real, physical apiary at a real address. ⚠

The Single Biggest Opportunity

Put "Rio Rancho" and "New Mexico" in your titles, and give nucs, queens, and honey their own pages.

You're a real local apiary with a real address — right now your site doesn't say where you are anywhere Google can read it. Fixing the title tags is a same-week job; splitting your five products across their own pages is what lets each one actually compete for its own search instead of all fighting for space on one page.

What I'd Fix In The First 2 Weeks

  1. 01Rewrite the homepage title to lead with "Rio Rancho, NM" instead of "Home |."
  2. 02Split Products & Services into dedicated Nucs, Queen Bees, and Honey pages.
  3. 03Write real meta descriptions targeting "bee nucs Rio Rancho" and "raw honey New Mexico."
  4. 04Add LocalBusiness schema so Google can show your address and hours directly.
  5. 05Add a dedicated Swarm Removal page — a free service you already offer that nobody can currently find.
