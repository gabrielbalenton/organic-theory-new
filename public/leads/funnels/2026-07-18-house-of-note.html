The Single Biggest Opportunity

Pick one canonical domain and redirect the old one into it.

65+ years of Twin Cities reputation is real proof — right now it's split between the clean, current houseofnote.com and an old www version still carrying a squished-word title bug from a past redesign. Consolidating onto one domain turns two competing versions into one authoritative site.

What I'd Fix In The First Week

  1. 01301-redirect every www.houseofnote.com page to its equivalent on houseofnote.com.
  2. 02Add a self-referencing canonical tag to every page so it's unambiguous which domain is authoritative.
  3. 03Confirm no page still carries the old "MinnesotaMinnesota" template anywhere else on the site.
  4. 04Resubmit the cleaned-up sitemap to Google Search Console so the old URLs drop out of the index faster.
  5. 05Add LocalBusiness schema so Google can show your hours and address as rich results.
