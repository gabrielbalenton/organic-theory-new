Growth Audit — Jackley Chiropractic

Your own homepage isn't sure who's talking.

Jackley Chiropractic has real, loyal patients in Independence — but the homepage copy switches from "we" to "they" mid-paragraph, and the footer's Text Us button doesn't actually text anyone.

What I Found On Your Site

01
✗

Your homepage doesn't know if it's "we" or "they"

The welcome paragraph opens in first person - "We are dedicated to staying on top of the latest chiropractic tools..." - then switches mid-paragraph to third person: "They are here to answer your questions... It is their goal... they are great at what they do!" It reads like directory copy that was never rewritten in your own voice.

02
✗

The "Text Us" button can't actually send a text

The footer's sticky "Text Us" link is coded as sms:chadjackley@yahoo.com - an email address where a phone number belongs. On most phones, tapping it either fails outright or opens a blank message with no valid recipient, so patients trying to text you can't.

03
⚠

Real patient trust isn't leading the page

Your testimonials describe patients who've stuck with Dr. Jackley for years and avoided surgery - genuinely strong proof - but it sits below the fold instead of backing up the homepage's opening pitch.
