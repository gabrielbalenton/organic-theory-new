Growth Audit — The Picture Framer Glasgow

Your bespoke framing page is competing with itself.

Google has two separate, live pages on pictureframerglasgow.com carrying almost word-for-word the same content about your Guild Commended framing service — splitting the ranking credit that should be going to one page.

What I Found On Your Site

01
✗

Two live pages for the same bespoke framing content

Google's index shows both pictureframerglasgow.com/our-services/bespoke-framing and pictureframerglasgow.com/pages/bespoke-framing indexed at once — same description of the five Fine Art Trade Guild framing levels (Museum, Conservation, Commended, Budget, Minimum) and the same bio for Lorna and Archie, word for word, living at two different URLs.

02
⚠

Split ranking signal instead of one strong page

When two URLs carry the same content, Google has to decide which one to show — and any links or trust either page earns gets divided between them instead of building up a single page that could rank higher on its own.

03
⚠

Guild Commended credentials buried in the duplication

Lorna and Archie's 25+ years of Fine Art Trade Guild Commended experience is a real differentiator against big-box framers — but it's harder for that credential to surface clearly in search when it's split across two competing pages.

The Single Biggest Opportunity

Merge the duplicate page into one canonical URL Google can actually rank.

Right now your bespoke framing content is split across two indexed URLs, each earning a fraction of the trust one page could hold. Picking a canonical URL and 301-redirecting the other means all of that ranking signal — and Lorna and Archie's Guild Commended credentials — finally live in one place.

What I'd Fix In The First Week

  1. 01Pick one canonical URL for bespoke framing and 301-redirect the other into it.
  2. 02Check the rest of the site for the same old-navigation-vs-new-Shopify-pages pattern and consolidate anything else duplicated.
  3. 03Add LocalBusiness schema so Shawlands Arcade hours and reviews can show directly in search.
  4. 04Submit a fresh XML sitemap so Google re-crawls the cleaned-up structure quickly.
  5. 05Pull in and display real Google reviews on the homepage.
