Growth Opportunity Report

North Wollongong Physiotherapy is ranking on Google — but losing patients before they even call.

Your clinic comes up in local searches, which means the hard work of being visible is already done. But three fixable issues on your site and Google listing are quietly sending potential patients to competitors.

What we found on your site

🔍

No meta description in Google search results

When someone searches "physio Wollongong" or "sports physio near me", your listing appears without a description. Google fills the gap with random text pulled from your page. That makes your result look unprofessional compared to competitors who show a clear, compelling reason to click. This alone is costing you clicks every day.

📞

Two different phone numbers on the same site

Your site currently shows 02 4238 7913 in one location and 02 4244 1493 in another. This inconsistency is called an NAP mismatch (Name, Address, Phone) and Google uses it as a trust signal. When your contact details don't match across your own site, it can quietly suppress your local search ranking. It also creates confusion for patients who notice it.

✏️

Hero headline doesn't convert first-time visitors

"We Understand" is the first thing visitors read. It's vague, and it doesn't tell someone in pain why they should book with you specifically. A headline built around outcomes ("Back pain relief in Wollongong, starting this week") can significantly lift the number of people who go from browsing to booking.

The single most impactful improvement

Fixing the phone number inconsistency and adding a tailored meta description will improve your local search ranking and your click-through rate at the same time. These are one-time fixes that compound over time as Google re-indexes your pages.

Realistic outcome within 60 days: higher position in "physiotherapy Wollongong" searches, more organic calls from patients who found you without a referral or paid ad.
