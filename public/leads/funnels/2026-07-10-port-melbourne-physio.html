Growth Audit — Port Melbourne Physiotherapy & Pilates

Your main nav links go nowhere.

Port Melbourne Physiotherapy & Pilates has real hours, two contact numbers, and a clear address — but your main navigation items don't actually link anywhere, and your cart icon is showing a literal placeholder.

What I found on your site

✗

Main navigation links are broken

Menu items like "Physiotherapy" and "Clinical Pilates" use "#" placeholder links instead of routing to their actual pages — a visitor clicking your main services from the nav bar goes nowhere.

⚠

Cart icon shows literal "[0]" placeholder

Instead of a working dynamic item count, the cart icon displays the raw placeholder text "[0]" — a visible bug live on the site right now.

⚠

No meta description or schema markup

Nothing tells Google what to show for physiotherapy searches in Port Melbourne, and no structured data for your two clinic phone numbers and hours.

⚠

Missing alt text across logo, blog, and product images

Logo images, blog post thumbnails, and your e-book product image all lack descriptive alt text.

The single biggest opportunity

Fix the broken navigation links and cart placeholder first — both are live bugs a visitor can see today. Then add schema markup so Google can show your hours and both phone numbers directly in search. You already have the real content (hours, address, services) — it just needs to actually be clickable and structured correctly.
