What we found on your site

🔍

Homepage title is a raw URL slug

Search Google for your salon and the result title reads "shinehairwellington/salon" — a URL-slug-style string, not your actual business name. Next to other Wellington salons with a real name and tagline, it looks unfinished.

📞

Same issue on your Contact page

Your Contact page — the page someone visits right before calling or booking — carries the same slug-style title. There's no confirmation in the listing that they've found the right salon.

✂️

A missed first impression

Hair salons compete on trust and style. A search result that reads like an unformatted URL undercuts that impression before someone has even seen your work.

Replacing the raw slug-style title with a real, benefit-led title ("Shine Hair Consultants & Design | Oriental Bay, Wellington") makes the listing look legitimate the moment it appears in search.

Realistic outcome within 30 days: better click-through from the local searches you're already appearing in — no extra ad spend, just a title that matches the quality of your work.
