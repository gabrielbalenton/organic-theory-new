Growth Audit — Ohio Roof Masters

Your homepage says "0 years serving Ohio."

Right now, live on your homepage, the stat counters that should read "7+ years serving Ohio" and "50+ years combined experience" are showing literal zeros. Every visitor sees it. So does Google.

What I found on your site

✗

Your stat counters are showing "0" in production

The homepage sections that should read your real numbers — years serving Ohio, years combined experience, office locations — currently display "0 +" and "0". For a roofing company where trust and track record are the whole pitch, this actively undermines credibility with every visitor.

✗

No meta description

Nothing tells Google what to show in search results for "roofer Troy Ohio" or your other 5 service areas — you're leaving your search snippet to chance.

⚠

No schema markup

No LocalBusiness or RoofingContractor structured data, so you're not eligible for the star-rating and business-info rich results that show up for competitors who have it set up.

⚠

Broken service-area link

Your "Troy" location link routes to a generic contact form instead of a dedicated Troy service page — a missed opportunity for local SEO and a confusing click for a visitor expecting location-specific info.

⚠

Before/after project photos have no alt text

Your strongest sales asset — real project photos — isn't helping your image search rankings or accessibility because none of them have descriptive alt text.

The single biggest opportunity

Fix the broken stat counters first — it's the fastest, highest-impact change on the page. Then add schema markup and a real location page for each of your 6 service areas. For a roofing company, trust signals and local relevance are what convert a homeowner mid-search into a booked estimate.
