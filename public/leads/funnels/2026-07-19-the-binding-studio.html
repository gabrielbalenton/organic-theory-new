The Single Biggest Opportunity

One page owning your name, not two.

Twenty years of solo craftsmanship is exactly the kind of story that should own the search for "hand bookbinder Auckland" outright — right now that authority is split between your homepage and a near-duplicate page carrying your own name twice in its title. Consolidating them is a same-week fix.

What I'd Fix In The First Week

  1. 01Redirect /the-binding-studio into your real homepage so there's one authoritative page for your studio name.
  2. 02Merge or clearly differentiate /shop-menu and /shop-materials so Google isn't choosing between two near-identical pages.
  3. 03Shorten the repeated "— The Binding Studio | Artisan Bookbinder" suffix so titles have room to mention West Auckland and Louise by name.
  4. 04Add LocalBusiness schema for the Waitakere Ranges location so local searches have something to match against.
  5. 05Resubmit the fixed pages in Search Console and track impressions for "hand bookbinder Auckland."
