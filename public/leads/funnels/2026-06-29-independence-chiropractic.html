Growth Audit — Independence Chiropractic

Your website is losing new patients before they ever call.

Independence Chiropractic in Celina, TX has a solid foundation — but three fixable issues on your site are creating friction that sends potential patients straight to a competitor.

What I found on your site

✗

Placeholder content is live on your homepage

One of your process sections still reads "23 Something" — visible to every visitor and every Google crawler. This signals an unfinished site and lowers both trust and search ranking.

✗

Your two "Book Appointment" buttons go to different pages

One CTA links to /appointment, another to /contact. A patient clicking the wrong one hits a form instead of a calendar — and most of them leave. Broken booking flow is one of the highest-cost issues a healthcare site can have.

✗

No way to capture visitors who aren't ready to book

Your site is all-or-nothing — either visitors book an appointment or they leave. There's no email opt-in, no free guide, no symptom quiz. You're losing every warm lead that needs a little more time.

⚠

Reviews section is empty despite being promised

The page header says "Reviews From Patients" but no reviews actually appear. For a health provider, social proof is everything — this gap actively reduces trust.

The single biggest opportunity

Fix the booking flow and add a lead capture mechanism. Right now, a patient searching "chiropractor Celina TX" lands on your site, can't tell where to click, and bounces. With a single embedded booking widget (Calendly or Jane App) and a simple "Free 15-min consult" opt-in, you could convert 20–40% more of those visitors into booked appointments.

What I'd fix in the first 2 weeks

  1. 1. Remove all placeholder content and audit every CTA link for consistency.
  2. 2. Embed a single booking widget above the fold — one button, one destination.
  3. 3. Add a simple lead magnet (free back pain guide or symptom quiz) with email capture.
  4. 4. Pull in real Google reviews via API and display them on the homepage.
  5. 5. Add local schema markup so you appear in Google's "chiropractor near me" results.
